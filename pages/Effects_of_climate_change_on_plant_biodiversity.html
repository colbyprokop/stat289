Effects of climate change on plant biodiversity

Environmental conditions play a key role in defining the function and distribution of plants , in combination with other factors. Changes in long term environmental conditions that can be collectively coined climate change are known to have had enormous impacts on current plant diversity patterns; further impacts are expected in the future. [1] It is predicted that climate change will remain one of the major drivers of biodiversity patterns in the future. [2] [3] [4]
