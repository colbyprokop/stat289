Environmental issue

Environmental issues are harmful effects of human activity on the biophysical environment . Environmental protection is a practice of protecting the natural environment on individual, organizational or governmental levels, for the benefit of both the environment and humans. Environmentalism , a social and environmental movement , addresses environmental issues through advocacy, education and activism. [1]
